Benefits of this system

Efficiency

Coaches can spend less time writing emails and focus on the most important thing: coaching!

Tracking

Anyone can view the league table or a players stats! Viewing player stats is a great benefit for any coach.

User-friendly

This website is simpler to use than the legacy system used at Oundle school which will make life easier.

A level coursework

This website was developed by Angelo Giacco to manage the EMIS water polo league for his Computer Science A level coursework. It will greatly improve the management of the water polo league and will allow stakeholders to easily check on the league table and statistcs. The languages used to developed this were HTML, CSS, JS and PostgreSQL. If you are interested in using this website for your own league do not hesitate to contact us.
